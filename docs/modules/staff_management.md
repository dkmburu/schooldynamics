# Staff Management Module  
**Purpose:** Centralized management of the entire staff lifecycle — recruitment, onboarding, active service, performance, and exit — for both teaching and non-teaching staff.

---

## 1. Staff Directory  
**Description:** Central register of all staff members.

### 1.1 [Tab] All Staff  
Displays a master list of all staff (teaching and non-teaching).  
Includes search, filters, status badges (Active, On Leave, Contract Ending), and quick actions (view, edit, suspend, archive).

### 1.2 [Tab] Teaching Staff  
Filtered view showing only teaching staff.  
Displays TSC status, subjects taught, classes assigned, and teaching load.

### 1.3 [Tab] Non-Teaching Staff  
Filtered view showing support and operational staff.  
Displays department, role, shift type, and assigned assets.

### 1.4 [Tab] Archived / Former Staff  
Read-only records for exited staff.  
Includes exit reason, clearance status, and historical employment data.

---

## 2. Recruitment & Hiring  
**Description:** Manages the hiring pipeline before applicants become staff.

### 2.1 [Tab] Job Positions  
Defines approved staff positions.  
Includes job title, department, staff category (teaching/non-teaching), required qualifications, and approval status.

### 2.2 [Tab] Job Advertisements  
Manages published vacancies.  
Links to job positions, application deadlines, and visibility (internal or public).

### 2.3 [Tab] Applications  
Applicant Tracking System (ATS).  
Stores applicant profiles, CVs, documents, and shortlisting status.

### 2.4 [Tab] Interviews & Assessments  
Structured evaluation of applicants.  
Supports panel scoring, interview notes, and recommendations.

### 2.5 [Tab] Hire & Convert to Staff  
Final recruitment stage.  
Generates offer letters, sets up contracts, and converts successful applicants into staff records.

---

## 3. Onboarding & Contracts  
**Description:** Formalizes staff engagement after hiring.

### 3.1 [Tab] Onboarding Checklist  
Tracks onboarding tasks such as document submission, system access, and orientation completion.

### 3.2 [Tab] Contracts  
Manages employment contracts.  
Includes contract type, start/end dates, renewals, and expiry alerts.

### 3.3 [Tab] Probation Management  
Tracks probation periods.  
Supports supervisor reviews and confirmation or extension decisions.

---

## 4. Staff Profiles  
**Description:** Detailed individual staff records.

### 4.1 [Tab] Personal Information  
Stores biodata including identity details, contact information, and emergency contacts.

### 4.2 [Tab] Employment Details  
Captures position, department, supervisor, duty station, and employment type.

### 4.3 [Tab] Qualifications & Skills  
Stores academic and professional qualifications, licenses, skills, and expiry tracking.

### 4.4 [Tab] Documents  
Secure document repository.  
Includes contracts, ID copies, certificates, and versioned uploads.

---

## 5. Academic Assignment (Teaching Staff)  
**Description:** Teaching-specific operational management.

### 5.1 [Tab] Subjects & Learning Areas  
Assigns CBC learning areas and subjects based on teacher qualifications.

### 5.2 [Tab] Classes & Streams  
Manages class teacher roles and stream assignments.

### 5.3 [Tab] Timetable & Workload  
Handles teaching periods, workload balancing, and overload detection.

### 5.4 [Tab] Co-Curricular Roles  
Assigns clubs, sports, houses, and other co-curricular responsibilities.

---

## 6. Operations & Roles (Non-Teaching Staff)  
**Description:** Operational and support staff management.

### 6.1 [Tab] Roles & Duties  
Defines functional responsibilities and task scopes.

### 6.2 [Tab] Shifts & Rosters  
Manages work schedules, shifts, rotations, and duty rosters.

### 6.3 [Tab] Assets & Equipment  
Tracks assets issued to staff such as laptops, vehicles, and tools.

---

## 7. Attendance & Leave  
**Description:** Time, presence, and availability management.

### 7.1 [Tab] Attendance Logs  
Tracks clock-in/clock-out records from manual or biometric systems.

### 7.2 [Tab] Leave Applications  
Manages staff leave requests with approval workflows and attachments.

### 7.3 [Tab] Leave Balances  
Automatically calculates leave entitlements, accruals, and usage history.

---

## 8. Performance & Appraisals  
**Description:** Staff evaluation and professional growth.

### 8.1 [Tab] Appraisal Cycles  
Defines appraisal periods and evaluation criteria.

### 8.2 [Tab] Performance Reviews  
Stores supervisor assessments, scores, and remarks.

### 8.3 [Tab] Classroom Observation (Teaching Staff)  
Records lesson observations, feedback, and improvement actions.

### 8.4 [Tab] CPD & Training  
Tracks training programs, CPD credits, and development needs.

---

## 9. Payroll & Benefits  
**Description:** Financial engagement with staff.

### 9.1 [Tab] Salary Structure  
Defines basic salary, allowances, and deductions.

### 9.2 [Tab] Statutory Details  
Manages PAYE, NSSF, NHIF, and other statutory information.

### 9.3 [Tab] Payroll History  
Read-only view of payroll runs, payslips, and payment dates.

---

## 10. Discipline & Compliance  
**Description:** Governance, ethics, and accountability.

### 10.1 [Tab] Incident Reports  
Logs complaints, misconduct cases, and violations.

### 10.2 [Tab] Disciplinary Actions  
Tracks warnings, hearings, decisions, and outcomes.

### 10.3 [Tab] Compliance Records  
Stores policy acknowledgements and code-of-conduct acceptance.

---

## 11. Exit & Clearance  
**Description:** Controlled and auditable staff exit process.

### 11.1 [Tab] Resignation & Termination  
Initiates staff exit with reasons and notice tracking.

### 11.2 [Tab] Clearance Checklist  
Tracks asset return, system access revocation, and departmental sign-offs.

### 11.3 [Tab] Exit Interviews  
Captures feedback, lessons learned, and improvement insights.

---

## 12. Reports & Analytics  
**Description:** Decision support and compliance reporting.

### 12.1 [Tab] Staff Statistics  
Headcount, teaching vs non-teaching ratios, gender and diversity metrics.

### 12.2 [Tab] Academic Staffing Reports  
Teacher-student ratios, subject coverage, and workload analysis.

### 12.3 [Tab] Contract & License Alerts  
Dashboards for expiring contracts, licenses, and certifications.

---

## 13. Settings & Configuration  
**Description:** System setup and governance.

### 13.1 [Tab] Departments & Units  
Defines organizational structure.

### 13.2 [Tab] Job Titles & Grades  
Normalizes job titles and grading structures.

### 13.3 [Tab] Leave Types & Policies  
Configures leave categories, limits, and rules.

### 13.4 [Tab] Roles & Permissions  
Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) mapping for staff system access.

---
